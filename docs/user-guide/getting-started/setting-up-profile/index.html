Setting Up Your Profile

After logging into VisionCloud, you can customize your profile settings to personalize your experience and manage your account information.

Profile information​

In the profile section, you can update:

  • Personal details: Edit your name, email address, and other contact information.
  • Profile picture: Upload or change your profile avatar that appears throughout the platform.
  • Display preferences: Customize the language of the interface.
  • Account settings: Manage your login credentials and security settings.

Click on Save to apply any changes you make to your profile.

Changing your password​

To change your password click on the Change password checkbox in the profile section. You will be prompted to enter your current password and then set a new password.
