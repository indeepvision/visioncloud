Quick Tour

This guide will help you discover VisionCloud in less than 5 minutes. You'll see how to log in and navigate the platform.

Log in​

Navigate to VisionCloud with your preferred browser and log in using your email and password. If you don't have an account yet, ask your organization administrator to invite you.

➡️ Access VisionCloud from https://cloud.id-vision.com

Select your organization​

The first time you login, you will have to select your organization from a list.

If you belong to multiple organizations, you can switch between them later. After the initial login, you will be taken directly to your last used organization.

Explore the home screen​

After selecting your organization, you will be taken to the Projects section, which is the home screen. This is the first page you will see when you open VisionCloud.

In the left there is the navigation sidebar, which has shortcuts to the latest projects you have opened, your profile settings, and the administration section.

In the Projects section, you'll see all the projects available within your organization. This is what you can do with here:

  • Filter projects using the Add Filter button.
  • Search for a specific project by text.
  • Display only projects within a certain date range.
  • Create a new project from scratch using the Create Project button.

Inside a project​

A project is a workspace to organize your datasets and models. Each project has its own datasets, models, members, and settings.

If you click on a project, you will enter the project view. From the top navigation bar you can see its datasets, models and configure the project in the Settings tab.
